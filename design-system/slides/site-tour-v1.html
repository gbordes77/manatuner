Overview

Two surfaces under one identity.

A calculator that does the math you'd skip, and a library that surfaces what the pros have already written.

The Calculator

Paste a decklist. See exactly when each spell becomes castable — turn by turn, on the play and on the draw. Hypergeometric math, no guessing.

The Library

54 curated resources from the canon of competitive MTG. Karsten, PVDDR, Saito, Chapin, Budde — one click away, organised by format intent.

03 / 08

The Calculator · Workflow

Three steps. A few seconds.

01

Paste your decklist

MTGA export, plain text, one card per line. Split cards, DFCs, sideboards and Arena set codes are auto-normalised. Scryfall validates every name.

02

The math runs in your browser

Hypergeometric distribution per turn × per mana cost. Monte Carlo mulligan over 10,000 hands. Bellman keep/mulligan thresholds. Nothing leaves your device.

03

Read the verdict

Castability % for every cost. Mulligan guidance. Ramp accounting. Post-board sideboard editor. Save the analysis locally — no signup, no account.

Hypergeometric · Frank Karsten · Bellman · Fisher–Yates

04 / 08

The Calculator · Outputs

Four lenses on your manabase.

Castability per turn

Probability of casting each mana cost on T1, T2, T3 and beyond — on the play AND on the draw.

Mulligan thresholds

Bellman keep/mulligan EVs for opening hands of 7, 6, 5 cards. London mulligan rules.

Ramp is mana too

13 producer types automatically detected: mana dorks, rocks, rituals, land auras, doublers, landfall, treasure…

Post-board analysis

Sideboard swap editor recomputes the manabase for the matchup you're prepping.
