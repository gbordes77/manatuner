05 · Principles

Three rules govern the visual language

01

Authenticity over decoration

The mana palette is canon — Plains cream, Island blue, Swamp near-black, Mountain red, Forest green. Never pastel-ize. Never desaturate. They are signal, not flair.

02

Knowledge as premium

The Library uses gradients normally reserved for high-tier products. Math content carries its own weight — discreet mono techTerm captions credit Karsten, Bellman, Karsten's tables instead of corporate jargon.

03

Quiet UI for a loud subject

The chrome stays sober — Inter body, Cinzel headings, parchment surfaces. The mana symbols and the WUBRG hero gradient do the talking. The card art is the moodboard.
