04 · Principles

01
Authenticity over decoration
WUBRG is the canon. Never pastel-ize, never desaturate. The mana colours are signal, not flair.
02
Knowledge as premium
The Library uses gradients reserved for high-tier products. Math content carries its own visual weight.
03
Quiet UI for a loud subject
Sober chrome (Inter, Cinzel, parchment). Mana symbols and the WUBRG hero do the talking.
